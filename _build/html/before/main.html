Before FOSS Starts¶

Please endeavor to complete the pre-FOSS setup before arriving at FOSS.

FOSS runs under a Code of Conduct. Please familiarize yourself with it.

FOSS Online sessions are recorded and recordings are posted on CyVerse’s private YouTube channel for participants to review at any time (only those with the link can view).

Need help?

Couldn’t find what you were looking for?

  • You can talk to any of the instructors or TAs if you need immediate help.

  • Chat with us on CyVerseFOSS Slack.
